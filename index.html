Kaffeepedia Enzyklopädie

A reference backbone for coffee knowledge

Inspired by the clarity of scientific encyclopedias and technical manuals, the Kaffeepedia Enzyklopädie documents varieties, processes, defects, water, extraction variables, and sensory language with precision.

Volumes
Arabica & Robusta A–Z
Genetic backgrounds, agronomy, cup profiles, processing compatibility, and roasting notes — structured for both farmers and roasters.
Structure
From molecule to cup
Entries link botany, chemistry, physics, and sensory outcomes instead of treating them as separate worlds.
Access
Multilingual & digital-first
Designed to live as a digital reference with cross-language navigation and deep search for professionals and learners.
Kaffeducation

From enthusiast to coffee scientist

Kaffeducation is the educational branch of Kaffeepedia — offering structured learning paths for baristas, roasters, green buyers, sensory analysts, and future coffee researchers.

Pathways
Foundation to advanced
Tiered modules covering fundamentals, intermediate practice, and advanced scientific perspectives on coffee.
Standards
Aligned with industry & beyond
Designed to complement existing certifications while going deeper into real science and measurement.
Formats
Text, visual, and interactive
Books, interactive content, and tools that help professionals apply concepts on bar, in lab, and in roastery.
Coffee Science

Physics, chemistry, and sensory — properly connected

Kaffeepedia treats coffee as a scientific system: mass, energy, flow, concentration, perception. The goal is to describe quality with transparent, testable concepts — not vague language.

Physics
Flow, heat, and extraction
Pressure, resistance, flow rate, temperature fields, and extraction curves expressed in readable, physically grounded models.
Chemistry
Compounds and transformations
From green composition to roast chemistry to brew composition, tied to measurable variables like TDS and EY.
Sensory
Psychophysics & perception
Linking instrument data and human perception, accounting for thresholds, adaptation, context, and cultural differences.
Coffee AI

A focused assistant for coffee, not general chat

The Kaffeepedia AI is designed as a disciplined, coffee-only assistant: it helps with extraction, roasting, sensory analysis, water, origin questions, and education — without noise.

Use cases
From bar to lab
Recipe design, troubleshooting shots, reading roast curves, interpreting refractometer data, and planning experiments.
Integration
Apps & tools
Designed to live inside Kaffeepedia and Kaffeducation apps as a serious tool for professionals and students.
Philosophy
Precision, not hype
Grounded answers, references where possible, and a focus on usable, testable recommendations in real coffee contexts.
